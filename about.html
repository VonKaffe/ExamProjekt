Who are we?

We were created in Back Swamp Texas by two eldery grandmothers who unfortunately passed away a week later. They names were Lemon-Anne and Lacy-Lou. They saw a need for a company that offered services to all the people out there eho didn't know anything at all about the subject.

After their demise the idea went on sale on craigslist as a way for the families to pay of their elderlies debts they have collected by playing poker and eating sandwiches. For a dollar and 99 cents the company was sold to us, three lucky students from Sweden.

Our Design Company is an experienced and passionate group of designers, developers, project managers, writers and artists. Every client we work with becomes a part of the team. Together we face the challenges and celebrate the victories.
